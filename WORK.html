This is what I do

Analysing data, developing insightful reports, creating beautiful designs and being part of heart-warming communities. These are things I've kept myself busy with and are things I absolutely love doing! Through my studies as game artist and data analyst I've gained an interesting variety of skills. This insight has proven to be quite valuable in my professional life. Not only can I think about the front-end, but also take note of the back-end of things. 

While it may not be immediately clear what my focus is, I can assure you I have one determination in work: Adding high value. 'Doing things half-baked' is not part of my dictionary. When I'm given an assignment I will execute it to the best of my capabilities. With my skillset and eagerness to learn that luckily doesn't mean I take longer than usual. On the contrary! With my wide knowledge and an out-of-the-box way of thought I may find a simpler solution to a large problem. This is a quality I'm most proud of. I'm always looking for solutions and like to apply these wherever possible.

Have I enticed you in learning more about the things I do? Feel free to scroll down and set your gaze on my proudest works!
